How much is your home Worth?

Call or write me today to get your free comparative home and market evaluation

When selling your home, an appraisal of the property's value is an important step in coming up with a listing price. This is one of the services your real estate agent offers. The agent looks at the price of properties in your neighborhood that sold within the past year or two and that are similar to your home in terms of size, features and condition. These properties are called "comparables." Then the agent adds or subtracts value to your home when considering features that might not be found in the comparables; for instance, a property fence adds value to your home's worth, but peeling exterior paint usually subtracts from it.
